Places to Visit in Kerala

Discover the stunning places to visit in Kerala, from serene backwaters to lush hill stations. Here are some must-visit places:

1. Munnar

Munnar is a hill station located in the Western Ghats. It is famous for its tea plantations, picturesque landscapes, and cool climate.

2. Alleppey

Known as the "Venice of the East," Alleppey is famous for its backwaters, houseboats, and scenic beauty.

3. Kochi

Kochi (Cochin) is a vibrant city known for its rich history, beautiful churches, and the famous Chinese fishing nets.

4. Wayanad

Wayanad is known for its lush greenery, wildlife sanctuaries, and beautiful waterfalls.

5. Kovalam

Kovalam is a popular beach destination known for its crescent-shaped beaches and vibrant nightlife.

6. Varkala

Varkala is known for its cliffs adjacent to the Arabian Sea and is famous for its beaches and natural mineral springs.

7. Thekkady

Thekkady is known for the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, where you can see a variety of wildlife, including elephants.

8. Kumarakom

Kumarakom is a beautiful backwater destination famous for its houseboat cruises and bird sanctuary.
